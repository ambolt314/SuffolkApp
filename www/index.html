FAQ

Here are some questions we get asked the most about applying to the University of Suffolk

What can I study here?

We offers many courses, specialising in health and social sciences. Our schools are:

  • Science, Technology and Engineering
  • Art, Design and Humanities
  • Law and Social Science

Where is UoS?

We have campuses across Suffolk, but our epicentre is Ipswich. We're at the Waterfront building about 10 minutes from the station, you can't miss us!

What's the social life like?

There are societies you can join but, as a new university, we need more and better ones. There are also great freshers events and we have the Summer Ball in May

What grades do I need to get in?

We accept three C grades at A-Level or equivalent as a minimum, but this depends on the course
