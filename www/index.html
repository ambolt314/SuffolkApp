About the university

Founded in August 2016, famous people linked to the university include Helen Pankhurst, Ed Sheeran and Bill Turnbull. They offer many courses of varying quality, with emphasis on Social Sciences. The president of the Students Union is Georgia Downs, and the vice-president is Amy Grant.
